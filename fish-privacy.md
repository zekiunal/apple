# Privacy Policy for Fish Tank Simulator

**Last Updated:** November 4, 2025
**Effective Date:** November 4, 2025

## Introduction

Zeki Ünal ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Fish Tank Simulator mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through the App.

**TL;DR:** We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. Your game data stays on your device. Period.

---

## Information We Do NOT Collect

Fish Tank Simulator is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, including but not limited to:

- ❌ Name, email address, or contact information
- ❌ Location data or GPS coordinates
- ❌ Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- ❌ Usage statistics or analytics data
- ❌ Gameplay behavior or session data
- ❌ Crash reports or diagnostic information
- ❌ Photos, contacts, or other device data
- ❌ IP addresses or network information
- ❌ Biometric data
- ❌ Any other personally identifiable information

**We have no servers.** There is no backend. Your data never leaves your device.

---

## Local Data Storage

### What Is Stored Locally

The App stores game progress data exclusively on your device, including:

- Fish information (species, names, levels, stats)
- Tank data (cleanliness, inventory, decorations)
- Game settings (sound preferences, notifications)
- In-App Purchase history (managed by Apple)

### How It's Stored

- All data is stored locally using iOS/watchOS secure storage mechanisms
- Data persists between app sessions
- Data is included in device backups (iCloud/iTunes)
- We cannot access this data—it remains entirely on your device

### Data Deletion

You can delete all game data by:
1. Deleting the App from your device, OR
2. Resetting game data within the App (if available)

Once deleted, data cannot be recovered unless restored from a device backup.

## Third-Party Services

### Apple StoreKit (In-App Purchases)

The App uses Apple's StoreKit framework for optional in-app purchases. When you make a purchase:

- Apple processes the transaction according to their [Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
- We receive only a transaction receipt to validate the purchase
- We do not receive or store your payment information
- Purchase history is managed by Apple through your Apple ID

### User Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications (e.g., baby fish born, tank needs cleaning). These notifications:

- Are generated and delivered locally by your device
- Do not transmit data to external servers
- Can be disabled in iOS/watchOS Settings
- Do not track or collect any information

**No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.**

## Permissions
