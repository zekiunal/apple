## ğŸ’ Idle & Offline Progression

### How It Works

When you close the app:
- Hunger continues to increase
- Tank cleanliness decreases
- Baby fish continue growing (if conditions met)
- Breeding timers continue

**Offline Calculation:**
- When you return, game calculates time elapsed
- Simulates hunger, growth, and decay
- Applies changes all at once
- Notification: "You were away for X hours"

**Important:**
- Fish can't die, but health/happiness will drop
- Babies may stop growing if conditions aren't met
- Returning to 100% hunger requires immediate feeding

## ğŸ“ Glossary

**Active Hours:** Time range when a species is most active and gains bonus XP when fed

**Alpha Female:** Selected female fish that initiates breeding after 2 minutes

**Baby Fish:** Fish under 80% size, cannot breed, grows 1% per 10 seconds

**Cleanliness:** Tank hygiene stat (0-100%), affects health and breeding

**Cooldown:** Mandatory wait time between repeated actions

**Fullness:** Inverse of hunger (Fullness = 100 - Hunger)

**Leadership Score:** Calculated value determining male leader and alpha female

**Mood:** Fish emotional state (Sick/Hungry/Happy/Neutral) based on stats

**Offline Simulation:** Background calculation of game progression while app is closed

**Primary Tank:** First tank (Tank 1), offers free fish additions

**Waste Level:** Accumulates from feeding, reduces cleanliness

**XP:** Experience points, gained from feeding, used to level up fish
